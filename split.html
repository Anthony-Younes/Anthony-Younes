Advice for Your Workout Routine
The best advice I can offer is to pursue activities that you enjoy the most and that feel right for you. This approach has worked wonders for me, and I'd like to share my workout split in case you'd like to give it a try.

Instructions
 Every set in this workout is performed until failure. If you feel like you have more energy after reaching failure, I suggest incorporating a drop set to maximize your gains.

Workout Routine


day 1

  1. Bench Press /or any variation of flat press
  2. Incline Press| any variation
  3. Flyes | any variation
  4. alternating bicep curls /or any variation of standing curls
  5. cable curls straight bar/or preacher curls
  6. hammer curls
  7. wrist curls & reverse curls /or any forearm workout
